Mission

  1. Achieve QUALITY and EXCELLENCE in Education.
  2. To network school communities for pooling, sharing and sustaining of resources.
  3. To create an ambience of mutual respect and cooperation.
  4. Provide opportunities for student collaboration, research and collective insights.
  5. Encourage students to imbibe our country's values, ethics and appreciate its rich cultural heritage.
  6. Nurture students to become environmentally conscious and responsible global citizens.
  7. Enhance professional competency and organize empowerment programs for teachers.

Vision

Empower schools to face emerging changes and challenges in Education, initiate BEST practices and teaching pedagogies, develop a platform to foster interaction among schools, respect diversity, promote tolerance, harmony, equity and inclusivity. Endeavour to synergise thought process and consequent actions of gen next.

Objective

Apropos to the aims of Sahodaya School Complex : The main objective of Sahodaya’s is to share innovative strategies and collaborate to achieve quality benchmark in school education by integrating rich experiences and best expertise to shape and sharpen the future of young minds of our country. It helps to keep abreast the teaching community with the latest advancement of educational and administrative developments by organizing Capacity Building Enrichment Programmes / Workshops / Seminars / Conferences and in-service training programmes. It also lays emphasis on promoting academic collaboration through teacher exchanges, common science fairs, joint seminars, workshops, trainings, exchanging ideas, creating and exchanging subject wise question banks etc. which encourage professionalism among teachers and educational innovativeness among institutions. This promotes inter-school collaboration through inter-school competition and activities and by inviting experts from the varied fields of education for discussions and conferences to bring out the desired changes. It also aids in bringing all committed educators on a common platform with the dual purpose of facilitating peer interaction as well as serving as an effective channel of communication between the schools & CBSE and furthermore helps to recognize the achievement of students, staff, teachers and Principals. Our premise is to prepare the children for academic excellence and greater social commitment, promoting scholastic, as well as, co-scholastic excellence by establishing a co-relation between academic and other scholastic and co-scholastic activities. The focus is to build on students strengths and give them opportunities for improvement and honing appropriate skills that build competencies for future readiness. Sahodayas aim at authentic and meaningful learning through integration of Arts / AI / Technology / Inclusive Education - A STEP TOWARDS BUILDING BRIDGES.
